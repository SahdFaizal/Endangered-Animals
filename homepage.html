Why we chose Arabian Gazelle?

The Arabian Gazelle is an animal at the risk of being endangered. Uncontrolled hunting and loss of habitat is causing Arabian Gazelle to be endgangered.

Where are they found?

They are found in desert regions and protected areas across Arabaian Peninsula.
